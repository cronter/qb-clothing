How to Purchase Clothes:

Make sure you press "Select to buy" button if you want to buy clothes (jacket, shirt, pants etc.) after you purchased items will be on your inventory

After confirm, choose YES if you want to package clothing in an outfit bag.

Equipping Clothes:

To equip purchased clothing, open inventory and use clothing or outfit bag.
